The 'Do Whatever' Moment 🍿 🥇
The Problem

A Category That
Didn't Exist Yet.

Poptein — India's first protein popcorn — walked into the LIT School live brand challenge with one massive disadvantage: nobody knew what protein popcorn was. The category didn't exist in the Indian consumer's mental map. You couldn't sell something people had never imagined needing.

"When you're creating a new category, your first job isn't marketing. It's education. And the fastest way to educate is to make people curious."

The LIT School challenge added another layer of pressure: this wasn't a slide deck presentation. It was a live sell — pitch, activate, and sell product in real-time, in front of judges and competitors. With an ₹80 MRP product that had to compete for attention against established snack brands.

🔥
The Strategy

Don't Sell. Create
a Scene.

The insight: in a crowded space, the loudest thing you can do is be completely honest and slightly absurd. We didn't build a polished booth. We built a moment.

🎭

Costume Guerrilla

Dressed in a full black costume and hit the floor holding a handmade sign: "Buy from Us and MAKE US Do WHATEVER." Instant crowd stopper. Zero ad spend.

🍿

Product as Proof

The pitch was simple: "Protein popcorn sounds weird. Taste it and tell us it doesn't work." Let the product handle the conversion. No jargon, no feature lists.

💰

Premium Positioning

Priced at ₹360 avg despite ₹80 MRP. Justified via experience, curiosity, and the novelty premium — charged for the discovery, not just the product.

The Execution

Real-Time. No
Safety Net.

01

The Hook

Walked into the challenge venue in full costume with a handwritten sign. The sheer audacity created a crowd within minutes. People stopped — not because of the product, but because of the person.

02

The Sample Close

Once people stopped, they got a taste. The product's quality did the heavy lifting. Protein popcorn that actually tastes good is a genuine novelty — reactions were organic and enthusiastic.

03

The Premium Sell

Made the purchase feel like being part of something first — "You're eating India's first protein popcorn right now." At ₹360, that story justified the premium effortlessly.

04

The Win

Sold 20 packs. ₹7,000 total revenue. 4.5× the MRP. Won the LIT School challenge. The judges' feedback: "Most memorable activation of the day."
