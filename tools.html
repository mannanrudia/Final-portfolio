The Process

How I Approach
a Project.

01 — Deep-Dive the Brief

Read through the brief thoroughly. Understand every angle, every constraint, every opportunity. Then close the laptop.

02 — Walk for Inspiration

Go out for a walk. Let the ideas breathe. Some of the best concepts come when you're not staring at a screen.

03 — Brainstorm & Bulletproof

If inspiration doesn't hit — NotebookLM and Claude become my brainstorm partners. Debate every idea, then ask both to bulletproof the concept until it's airtight.

04 — Find Inspo & Storyboard

Once the idea clicks — dive into Instagram and YouTube for reference. Build a visual storyboard. If I hit a wall, use Nano Banana to generate rough visual frames and unlock the flow.

05 — Script & Refine

Get into the flow of writing the script. Refine it using Claude. Ask it to generate a detailed shot list with all the data compiled — locations, angles, timing, transitions.

06 — Produce & Edit

Execute the shoot, then edit using Premiere Pro and After Effects. Generate any impossible-to-shoot footage using Higgsfield. Colour grade in Lightroom. Ship it.
