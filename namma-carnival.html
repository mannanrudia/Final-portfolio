The Concept

Namma = "Ours."
This Was Bangalore's Own.

In a city bubbling with content creators but starved of genuine community, Namma Creator Carnival was built to fill that gap. Not another brand-pushed influencer event — a creator-first conclave that celebrated the Bangalore creator ecosystem on its own terms.

We raised ₹1.5 Lakhs to bring this vision to life — securing sponsorships, locking in the venue, and curating every element of the event from scratch. No playbook. No precedent. Just a vision and the hustle to make it real.

"10 of Bangalore's most influential creators. One stage. Zero corporate awkwardness. Just raw creative energy — and one host keeping it all alive."

Presented by Aniglow Professional and powered by The LIT School, the event was architected to create genuine connections between creators, not just photo opportunities.

The Strategy

Community First.
Content Follows.

The event strategy was built on a simple truth: authentic community drives organic content. Instead of forcing creators to perform for the brand, we created an environment where they naturally generated content just by being there together.

🎯

Hyper-Local Targeting

"Namma" (meaning "ours" in Kannada) was a deliberate signal — this wasn't a national event. It was Bangalore's own, for Bangalore's creators. Local identity as brand hook.

🤝

Creator-First Design

No brand speeches. No forced collabs. Just a curated, high-energy environment where Bangalore's biggest creators naturally connected, shared, and created together.

📲

Organic Content Loop

Every creator shared the event organically. 10 creators posting = instant reach to their combined audience with zero ad spend. Community-generated virality.

🎟️
The Execution

From Idea to
Sold-Out Room.

01

Curation

Handpicked the 10 most culturally relevant creators in Bangalore — not just by follower count, but by their real community influence, content quality, and audience trust.

02

Partnership Lock-in

Secured Aniglow Professional as the title sponsor and The LIT School as the power partner — aligning the event with India's top creative education brand.

03

Countdown Content Strategy

Built a pre-event hype machine — countdown posts, collaborator reveals, and teaser Reels. Each creator announcement was its own mini-moment of organic reach.

04

Live Hosting

Mannan took the mic as official host — energising the room, moderating creator conversations, and keeping the energy at a concert pitch throughout the event.

05

Post-Event Amplification

18 posts published. 202 followers organically. Group photos with 350+ likes. Each creator's share multiplied the event's reach across their combined Bangalore audience.
